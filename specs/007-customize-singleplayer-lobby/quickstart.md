# Quickstart: Singleplayer Lobby Customization

## Prerequisites
- Run the app locally (existing instructions in README).

## Manual Verification

### Scenario 1: Singleplayer lobby controls
1. Create or join a lobby as leader.
2. Switch to singleplayer.
3. Confirm the share URL control is not shown.
4. Click the difficulty button repeatedly and verify it cycles easy → medium → hard → easy.

### Scenario 2: Manage opponents overlay
1. Open the Manage Opponents overlay in singleplayer.
2. Confirm at least one opponent slot is present.
3. Change the opponent name and color; verify the changes stick immediately.
4. Add opponents up to 3; verify a fourth cannot be added.
5. Remove all opponents and confirm zero opponents is allowed.

### Scenario 3: Multiplayer unchanged
1. Switch to multiplayer.
2. Confirm share URL and existing difficulty buttons appear and behave as before.
